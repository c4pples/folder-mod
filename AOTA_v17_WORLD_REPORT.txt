AOTA v0.17 - Global Settlement Pass

This pass clarifies the non-European world to better match the mod's lore:
- Germany retains a limited colonial footprint through Qingdao, Togoland, and New Cameroon, while also keeping claims in East Africa.
- Britain and France remain major imperial powers, but both are overstretched and increasingly brittle.
- The Ottoman Empire still holds Baghdad, Basra, Damascus, Aleppo, and Jerusalem in 1936, making the Middle East a major unresolved crisis zone.
- The Raj survives as a formal imperial structure, but it starts the game with instability and a dedicated focus tree.
- Japan is framed as opportunistic rather than dominant, with setup aimed at treaty-port leverage and future expansion.
- The United States begins more internationally withdrawn than triumphant, helping justify its weaker external posture before internal collapse.
- Canada, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, Egypt, Malaya, Indonesia, and Portugal now have clearer starting setups tied to the world lore.
