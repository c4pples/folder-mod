AOTA v24 Major OOB Pass I

Scope:
- Germany
- Britain
- Russia
- Ottomans

What this pass adds:
- Custom land OOB files under history/units
- Distinct starting division templates for each major
- Country history rewired to use custom AOTA land OOB names

Design notes:
- Germany: professional infantry core, border security, cavalry screen, experimental light mobile formation
- Britain: compact home army, imperial garrisons, small mobile column
- Russia: uneven rifle army, frontier divisions, guard detachment, cavalry screen
- Ottomans: Anatolian core divisions, provincial security formations, Levant mobile column

Notes:
- This pass focuses on land OOB only.
- Existing naval OOB references remain unchanged.
- Templates are intentionally flavor-first rather than fully optimized meta builds.
