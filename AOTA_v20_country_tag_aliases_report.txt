AOTA country_tag_aliases pass

What this does:
- adds a safe common/country_tag_aliases layer
- creates stable semantic aliases for frequently referenced AOTA states
- helps future event/focus/decision scripting avoid brittle hardcoded tag assumptions

What it does NOT do:
- it does not replace country_tags definitions
- it does not change ownership or history by itself
- it does not magically fix undefined tag references elsewhere

Aliases added:
- OTT -> TUR
- DNB -> AUS
- IND -> RAJ
- USC -> ACC
- USR -> AWU
- USN -> ANA
- USP -> APF
- USM -> ACG
- USS -> ASL
- USX -> CCW
- CNG -> COG
- ALGX -> ALG
- UKX -> UKR
- IRL -> IRE
- FNX -> FIN

Why this helps:
- gives the mod a cleaner script vocabulary for the Ottoman state, Danubian state, Raj, and American splinters
- reduces the temptation to reuse KR-specific convenience tags later
- makes future expansion passes easier to read and maintain
