AOTA v27 Major Flavor + Leaders Pass I

Scope:
- Germany
- France
- Britain
- Austria-Hungary
- Russia
- Ottomans
- United States
- Italy

What this pass adds:
- New major flavor localisation file with thematic country intro text for the core majors
- Expanded leader description coverage for major starting leaders
- New character expansion file adding flavor-first advisors/figures for Germany, France, Austria-Hungary, the USA, and Italy
- Advisor names, titles, and descriptions written to match the mod's draw-peace world thesis

Design intent:
- Reinforce a unified narrative voice across the majors
- Make political paths feel rooted in social crisis rather than abstract ideology selection
- Give the player stronger thematic context for who these regimes are and what kinds of people speak for them

Notes:
- This is a flavor-and-presentation pass, not a mechanical rebalance
- New advisors are defined conservatively using traits already common in the mod
- Country intro keys are provided for use in future intro events, tooltips, or country-selection flavor
