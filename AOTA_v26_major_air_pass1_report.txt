AOTA v26 Major Air Pass I

Scope:
- Germany
- Britain
- Russia
- Ottomans
- France
- Austria-Hungary
- United States
- Italy
- Japan

What this pass adds:
- set_air_oob wiring in country history for all nine majors covered by the OOB passes
- custom air OOB files under history/units
- seeded fighter, tactical bomber, and limited naval bomber wings
- includes the pass-II land OOB content for France, Austria-Hungary, USA, Italy, and Japan as the working base

Design notes:
- Germany: strong central fighter nucleus plus tactical support
- Britain: home-defense fighter groups and a coastal naval-recon element
- Russia: larger but uneven fighter arm with a frontier detachment
- Ottomans: modest Anatolian air core and Levant detachment
- France: defensive fighter concentration with Rhine cover
- Austria-Hungary: small imperial air arm focused on surveillance and internal security
- USA: underexpanded but credible pursuit force and training detachment
- Italy: compact modernizing air arm with Alpine cover
- Japan: aggressive fighter concentration plus a naval detachment

Notes:
- This is a best-effort scripted air seed pass and has not been engine-tested here.
- Naval OOB references were left unchanged.
- Air wings are intentionally modest and flavor-first rather than fully optimized.
